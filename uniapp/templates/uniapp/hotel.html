Hotel Solution

The hotel serves as a prime destination for travelers, offering essential transit points and places of relaxation. In today's digital age, alongside advancements in transportation systems and a rising demand for accommodation, the hospitality industry is flourishing. Ensuring a comfortable environment for guests has never been more crucial.

By addressing critical security challenges, UNV hotel security solution creates a safer environment for guests and staff, ultimately enhancing the overall guest experience and reinforcing the hotel's reputation for safety and reliability.

Optimizing duty performance for better hotel operation

Effective staff monitoring is crucial for ensuring adherence to safety protocols and optimizing duty performance. UNV offers advanced solutions like No Work Clothes Detection, On-duty Detection and Sleep-on-duty Detection, which are essential for maintaining seamless hotel operations.

All-round protection,every detail from the entrance to the elevator

UNV hotel security solution offers comprehensive protection by continuously monitoring key areas such as corridors and elevators. It facilitates real-time monitoring and instant alarms for incidents such as smoking, fighting, or people falling, enabling prompt incident responses to ensure the safety of guests property.

More enjoyable and worry-free experience for guests

Customer flow counting can help hotels optimize staffing, improve processes like check-in and check-out, reduce wait times and improve overall guest convenience. In order to provide a better customer experience, we provide VIP notification function and can protect user privacy through privacy mask and face mosaic.
