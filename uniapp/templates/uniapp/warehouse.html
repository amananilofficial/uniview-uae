Warehouse and Logistics Solution

Business and consumers around the world depend on warehousing and logistics for safe and timely delivery of goods. Theft, break-ins and other unforeseen threats can have a devastating impact on their business continuity.

UNV’s end-to-end warehouse and logistics solution empowers operators to create a secure environment safeguarding employees, vehicle processes, and cargo storage. This ensures uninterrupted workflow and enhances operational efficiency.

Personnel

Protect employees and achieve efficient personnel management

Vehicle

From entry to exit, all key aspects of the vehicle's operation are guarded

Cargo

Monitor and record the condition and movement of all goods

Process

Ensure that every link of the operation is orderly and compliant

Perimeter Protection

Effective perimeter protection serves as the primary defense against security threats such as break-ins, vandalism, and theft. UNV's robust Tri-guard series and advanced thermal cameras swiftly detect intrusions, fires, and other abnormal, ensuring timely response and safeguarding your premises.

What We Offer

- Smart Intrusion Prevention with high alarm accuracy
- Active Deterrence to intervene proactively
- Thermal Technology to make the invisible visible

Access Control Management

In a bustling warehouse environment with heavy traffic and constant movement of people and products, efficient access control is crucial. UNV offers advanced access control system to facilitate the smooth passage of employees during peak hours and seamless vehicle movements around the clock. Additionally, our visitor management system enhances security by ensuring safe entry and exit for outsiders.

What We Offer

- UNV deep learning algorithm for high accuracy
- ANPR for smooth and automated vehicle access
- Access data available for query and attendance management

Cargo Management:

A huge warehouse has to store thousands of goods, the storage and transportation process of goods is the key to the business. UNV professional product line can meet the requirements of each link. In addition, thermal imaging technology can help monitor the condition of goods, detect abnormal temperatures and fire hazards promptly, and reduce losses.

What We Offer

- 360° panoramic view, a wide coverage without blind spot
- Clear image in backlight environment to record every cargo
- Temperature Measurement and Fire Detection
