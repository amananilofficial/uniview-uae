School Solution

The importance of school security solution cannot be overstated. A comprehensive security system fosters a safe and supportive environment, allowing students to concentrate on learning without safety concerns. By implementing a reliable security solution, schools can proactively address security challenges and enhance the peace of mind of parents.

UNV‘s school solution integrates video management, access control, time and attendance management, and vehicle management. Additionally, we offer professional educational interactive Flat Panels to support teaching activities, creating an atmosphere conducive to both safety and personal growth.

Intelligent analysis enables swift response to accidents

UNV School Solution offers schools all-day, full-coverage, high-definition surveillance that ensures clear and accurate footage even in dim or dark environments. Equipped with deep learning technology, our solution helps prevent safety hazards by identifying students entering restricted areas and analyzing behaviors to detect incidents such as falls, fights, smoking, and suspicious individuals wandering around. This enables security personnel to respond quickly and prevent incidents from escalating.

Ensuring safe student arrivals and departures

UNV Multiview Dual-lens PTZ Camera, combined with advanced people and vehicle access control system,  safeguard the entrance and exit of the campus. By monitoring and controlling campus access, we effectively prevent unauthorized entry, and any abnormal after-hour access will be recorded and trigger an alarm. Personnel access data will be recorded in detail for seamless retrieval and attendance management.
- Rapid and accurate personnel access control with multiple authentication methods
- ANPR for smooth and automated vehicle entry and exit

UNV InstaHub brings inspiration to teaching

The InstaHub series of smart interactive displays has extraordinary audio-visual performance, and is compatible with various educational software resources. It also allows combination of online and offline teaching, leading a new era of teacher-student interaction.
- Touchscreen makes it fluent to write, modify and save
- Dual operating systems allow more software to run on the smart interactive display by using OPS
- All-in-one design helps the classroom save space and provides greater flexibility
