Stadium Solution

A Stadium, as a bustling venue, faces a myriad of threats, including overcrowding, unruly behavior, and potential violence. Effective security measures are essential not only to safeguard fans and athletes, but also to uphold the reputation of the stadium itself.

By integrating UNV’s advanced technology into your operations, you can achieve unparalleled peace of mind. Our AIoT devices significantly reduce response times and enhance situational awareness, ensuring that your stadium is always a safe place for all attendees.

Robust perimeter protection with Tri-guard technology

The perimeter of a sports stadium is typically extensive and requires a large number of security personnel to cover it, which may cost a lot of money and manpower. Effective perimeter protection is crucial for preventing intrusions, break-ins, and other security threats. With deep-learning algorithms, UNV's Tri-guard series swiftly detects intruders and makes light and sound warnings, ensuring a rapid response to safeguard your premises.

Embrace the large scene with MultiView and OmniView

MultiView cameras can realize multi-directional monitoring and seamlessly combine panoramic views with details, making them the best choice for wide sports fields. OmniView cameras provide a wider field of view with a single installation, eliminating blind spots in the stadium. Choose MultiView and OmniView cameras to optimize security across your sports venue, ensuring unparalleled coverage and cost-effectiveness.

Ensure smooth flow of foot and vehicle traffic

Managing stadium entrances with advanced access control system helps fans enter venues quickly and smoothly without long queues, adding access control devices in specific areas to ensure that only authorized personnel can enter the area.
- Support multiple authentication methods including card, password, QR code, etc.
ANPR ensures smooth and automatic entry and exit of vehicles in parking lots, and offer effective parking guidance through parking space detection, maintaining traffic flow during peak hours.
- Effective parking guidance with parking spaces detection and license plates recognition
- 24-hour parking lot monitoring with vivid full-color images even in dim night
