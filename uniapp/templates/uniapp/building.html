Building Solution

Building is a venue for social activities such as economic, productive, cultural and entertainment activities. A comprehensive and sophisticated building security solution should take the specific social activities and personal behavior into consideration. It needs to protect the privacy of individuals, and meanwhile be able to cover all critical areas without any blind spot.

Uniview has been dedicating to the security industry for years, accumulated experience and knowledge on varied building scenes, and presented comprehensive building security solutions which can be applied to all kinds of building surveillance scenes.

High integration compatibility with third-party platforms

Fusion and visualization of events and data

Clear design and Simplified operation

Flexible integration of various modules to meet your needs

Reliable External Protection

- Realize multi-directional monitoring and seamlessly combine panoramic views with details with MultiView camera
- Smart Intrusion Prevention, significantly reduce false alarm, accurately focus on human, motor vehicle and non-motor vehicle
- Deterrence through warning lights and speakers, intervention in advance to prevent potential risks.

Digital Internal Upgrade

- Detailed monitoring in areas such as elevators, corridors, and warehouses
- Digital meeting room equipped with UNV interactive display products to foster interactivity and engagement
- Information display uses LCD video wall or LED to provide a perfect visual experience
- Smoking detection, fire detection, obstructed evacuation route detection, providing better and safer working environment

Unified Personnel Management

- Personnel are managed uniformly through departmental organizations
- Enhancing entry & exit with rapid and accurate staff identification
- Pleasant visitor experience through convenient on-site or remote registration
- Flexible attendance rules and attendance record management

Efficient Vehicle Management

- ANPR for smooth and automated vehicle entry and exit
- Effective parking guidance with parking spaces detection and license plates recognition
- 24-hour parking lot monitoring with vivid full-color images even in dim night
- Easy access and query of detailed vehicle traffic records

Centralized Management Platform

- Customize the modules you need (CCTV, Access Control, Video Intercom) to protect your building
- Rich data visualization and electronic map services
- Centralized and digitalized management platform improve efficiency
- Open integration with third-party platforms
