Shopping Mall Solution

A shopping mall is a vibrant hub of activity, comprising large buildings or interconnected structures that house a variety of shops, stores, restaurants, and entertainment venues. It’s a destination where people come to shop, dine, and enjoy leisure activities. Ensuring a secure shopping experience for consumers and facilitating smooth operations for store owners to boost revenue are top priorities for any mall.

We specialize in providing comprehensive security solutions tailored for shopping malls worldwide. Our integrated systems have been implemented in lots of malls globally, reinforcing our commitment to creating safe and enjoyable experiences for all. Discover how UNV's solution can elevate security and operational efficiency in your mall today.

Ensure Shopper Safety

Protect shoppers from theft, vandalism, and other security threats

Enhance Operational Efficiency

Streamlined security operations that allow store owners to focus on their business

Drive Higher Sales

By creating a safe and welcoming environment, we help increase foot traffic and encourage longer stays

Protect every area and corner of your shopping mall

Find our diverse range of products designed to fit every area of the shopping mall, from outdoor spaces and entrances to atriums, elevators, escalators, and warehouses. Capture every moment with high-definition video, ensuring clarity whether day or night, panoramic or detailed, without missing any details.

Master the customer flow data across each area of the mall

By understanding foot traffic patterns, shopping malls can optimize their layout to enhance flow and improve customer experience. Data on peak traffic times helps in efficiently allocating staff resources, ensuring adequate customer service and security. This helps enhance operational efficiency, improve customer satisfaction, and drive revenue growth through informed decision-making.

UNV display & control products brings immersive experience

In shopping malls, visibility is key to showcasing quality products and conveying essential messages. UNV offers colorful LED and LCD solutions for different scenarios. Whether highlighting star products on mall exteriors or at checkout counters to tell your brand story, or providing safety information and emergency notices promptly in inner mall corridors, our displays ensure optimal communication and customer engagement.
